class Box¶

Located in Src/C_BaseLib/Box.H and Src/C_BaseLib/Box.cpp.

A Box is an abstraction for defining discrete regions of c:macro:BL_SPACEDIM-dimensional indexing space. Boxes have an IndexType, which defines IndexType::CELL or IndexType::NODE based points for each direction and a low and high IntVect which defines the lower and upper corners of the Box. Boxes can exist in positive and negative indexing space.
